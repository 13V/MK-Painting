Frequently Asked Questions — Painters West Lakes

What paint is best for West Lakes homes near the water? +
We recommend Dulux Weathershield MAX or Haymes UltraShield for West Lakes exteriors. These are salt and humidity-resistant systems that resist blistering, peeling, and chalking — critical for lakeside and coastal-adjacent properties.
How much does exterior painting cost in West Lakes? +
Exterior painting in West Lakes ranges from $4,000–$10,000 depending on size, cladding type, and prep. Weatherboard homes require more prep. We provide free, detailed on-site quotes — call 0405 352 932.

Yes — extensive experience with weatherboard homes throughout West Lakes, Henley Beach, Semaphore, and Grange. We use thorough caulking, priming, and flexible top coats built for the coastal climate.
