Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our painting services

How long does a typical interior job take? +
Most residential interior painting projects (3-4 rooms) take between 3 to 5 days, depending on the level of preparation required.
Do you move the furniture for us? +
Yes! We assist with moving larger items is necessary. We also cover all furniture and flooring with professional-grade drop sheets to ensure total protection.
What type of paint do you use? +
We primarily use premium Australian brands like Dulux, Haymes, and Wattyl. These provide the best coverage and durability for the Adelaide climate.
Are you insured and licensed? +
Absolutely. MK Painting Services is fully licensed and carries comprehensive public liability insurance for your peace of mind.
