Choosing the Perfect Exterior Colors for Mawson Lakes Homes

Mawson Lakes is unique. Its modern architectural landscape, characterized by sleek lines, rendered finishes, and proximity to the water, requires a different approach to exterior painting than the historic suburbs of Adelaide. As specialized painters in Mawson Lakes, we’ve painted countless homes in this beautiful area, and we’ve noticed a few trends that not only look spectacular but also withstand the South Australian climate.

1. Modern Neutrals are King

Most homes in Mawson Lakes lean towards contemporary or "Mid-Century Modern" styles. For these builds, a palette of layered neutrals often works best. Think Dulux Lexicon Quarter for trims paired with a deeper Colorbond Monument or Dune for the main render. These colors create a sophisticated contrast that highlights the architectural features of your home.

2. Considering the Lake Effect

The humidity and salt-air near the Mawson Lakes waterways can affect how paint cures and ages. We always recommend premium, UV-resistant coatings like Dulux Weathershield. These products are engineered to resist peeling and fading, which is crucial when your home is exposed to the direct afternoon sun over the water.

3. Don't Fear the Feature Wall

One of the best ways to make a Mawson Lakes build stand out is through a strategically placed exterior feature wall. Deep charcoals or even organic timber-look stains on porticos can add massive curb appeal and significantly increase your property value.
