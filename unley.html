Everything you need to know about painting your Unley property

Do you paint bluestone and sandstone homes in Unley? +
Yes — Unley has an exceptional concentration of bluestone cottages, sandstone villas, and character homes from the Victorian and Federation eras. We have deep experience in preparing and painting these surfaces correctly, including proper masonry primers, breathable coatings, and heritage-appropriate colour selections that complement the natural stone.
How much does exterior painting cost for a character home in Unley? +
Exterior painting for a character home in Unley typically costs $3,500–$10,000 depending on size, condition of existing paint, amount of timber detailing, and height access required. Interior repaints start from $2,200. We provide free on-site quotes — call 0405 352 932 to book.
Do I need City of Unley approval to change my home's exterior colour? +
It depends on whether your property is heritage listed or within a heritage precinct. Many Unley homes fall within heritage zones where significant colour changes may require a development application. We regularly advise homeowners on period-appropriate palettes to keep approval processes simple — call us to discuss your specific property.
Are you licensed and insured to paint in Unley? +
Yes. M&K Painting Services holds builder's licence BLD251492, carries $20 million public liability insurance, and is a member of the Master Painters Association. We work regularly across Unley, Malvern, Hyde Park, Clarence Park, and surrounding inner-south suburbs.
