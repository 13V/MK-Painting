Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical painting project take?

Most single-room projects take 1-2 days, while a full interior or exterior can take 5-10 days depending on size and weather conditions. We always provide a clear timeline in our quote.

What brand of paint do you use?

We primarily use premium Dulux and Haymes paints for their durability and superior finish in South Australia's climate. However, we are happy to use your preferred brand if you have a specific requirement.

Are you insured and licensed?

Yes, absolutely. We hold South Australian building license BLD251492 and carry $20M in Public Liability Insurance for your peace of mind.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation quotes across the greater Adelaide area. We can often provide a rough estimate via photos, but a site visit ensures the most accurate pricing.
