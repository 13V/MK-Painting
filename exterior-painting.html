Quality Materials

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does exterior painting take in Adelaide?

+

Most exterior painting projects in Adelaide take 3-7 days depending on the size of your home and weather conditions. We provide realistic timeframes during our initial assessment and keep you informed throughout the process.

What's the best time of year for exterior painting in Adelaide?

+

Autumn and spring are ideal for exterior painting in Adelaide, offering mild temperatures and lower humidity. However, we work year-round and adapt our techniques to weather conditions to ensure quality results.

How often should I repaint my home's exterior in Adelaide?

In Adelaide's climate, most homes need exterior repainting every 7-12 years. Factors like sun exposure, weather conditions, and paint quality affect this timeframe. We can assess your home's condition during our consultation.
