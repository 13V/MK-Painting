How long does office painting take in Edwardstown? +
A typical office fitout repaint in Edwardstown takes 1–3 days depending on the size of the space. We often work after-hours or in sections to avoid disrupting your staff. We'll provide an accurate timeline with your free quote — call 0405 352 932 to book a site visit.
Do you use low-VOC paints for occupied office spaces? +
Yes — for occupied office spaces and workplaces where fumes are a concern, we use low-VOC paints that dry quickly and have minimal odour.
